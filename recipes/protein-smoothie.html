Vanilla Berry Protein Smoothie

Pink berry smoothie

Great refreshment for hot summer days. Packed with protein and sweet, healthy fruit! Good for weight and muscle gain.

Ingredients

  • 360 mL water, yogurt, or milk of choice
  • 1 scopp vanilla protein powder
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1/2 cup frozen raspberries
  • 1/2 cup frozen blackberries

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients into blender and mix until smooth
  2. Enjoy!
